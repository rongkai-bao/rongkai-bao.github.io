Tralomethrin (TRA), a synthetic pyrethroid insecticide, has long-term adverse effects on aquatic organisms, highlighting the necessity for effective methods to mitigate its toxicity. This study investigated the degradation efficiency of TRA using ultraviolet (UV) irradiation, ozone (O3) oxidation, and a combined O3/UV process.
