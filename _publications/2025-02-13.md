Benfuracarb is widely utilized for crop protection due to its effective pest control properties; however, little information is available regarding its adverse effects and possible molecular mechanisms in fish development. In the present study, benfuracarb exposure caused defects in the development and inflation of the swim bladder, as well as in the lipid metabolism of zebrafish larvae.
